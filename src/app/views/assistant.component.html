Benefits of boiler
maintenance and servicing

Spending money on a boiler that’s never given you any trouble may seem like an unnecessary expense.

Service for Safety

Carbon monoxide is odourless, colourless and tasteless, a silent killer which claims the life of around one person a week in the UK and makes up to 200 a year seriously ill.

Service for Comfort

We’ve all had that thought, when it’s freezing outside and we’re peering out at the snow, wondering what it would be like if, for some unforeseen reason, the boiler broke down.

Service for Efficiency

A serviced boiler makes for a more energy-efficient boiler. Engineers will inspect the boiler for faults with operating pressures and parts, ensuring there’s nothing preventing your boiler from operating at maximum efficiency.
